Additionally, I am keen on harnessing advanced machine learning techniques to address astrophysical challenges. For more information, please visit my Research page.

Recently, together with Sihao Cheng (IAS) and Eritas Yang (Princeton), we discovered an exceptional trans-Neptunian object (TNO) 2017 OF201. The most striking feature of 2017 OF201 is that its orbit does not following the apsidal clustering pattern observed in many other extreme TNOs. This pattern is thought to be caused by the gravitational influence of a massive planet beyond Neptune, or so-called Planet 9 or Planet X. The existence of 2017 OF201 might suggest that Planet 9 or X doesn't exist. Check out our paper for more details. This work is featured in the IAS and Princeton University websites, as well as in New York Times, Sky & Telescope, Phys.org, New Scientist, Universe Today, and many more.
